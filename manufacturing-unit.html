Manufacturing Unit

Organized manufacturing built for formulation flexibility, hygiene, and output confidence.

The manufacturing setup is designed around separate process wings, batch discipline, filling flexibility, packaging support, and scalable production handling across multiple agro-chemical categories.

Production Story

Focused layout, controlled wings, and packaging readiness across categories.

The unit is organized to support insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, plant growth promoters, flowering stimulants, and allied crop-care products through carefully planned manufacturing and packaging operations.

Separate Wings

Product handling is planned through separate wings to reduce cross-contamination risks and keep formulation flow more controlled.

Flexible Formulations

The facility is suited for multiple formulation formats including EC, EW, SL, WP, SP, WDG, DF, SC, and CS style processing workflows.

Batch Hygiene

Material-contact areas and vessel discipline are planned with hygiene and stability in mind to support cleaner batch execution.

Packaging Support

Labels, sleeves, cartons, wrapping, strapping, and coding support help complete the product journey from production to dispatch.
